Recognized tags:

verbs:
-transitive - takes a direct object, otherwise assumed to be intransitive
-auxiliary - takes a complementary infinitive
-forward - unable to be expressed continuously or in the past; disallows the imperfect tense, the past tense, any English translation ending in -ing

nouns:
-proper - a proper noun; capitalizes the first letter and disallows articles
-uncountable - unable to be plural




misc:

adjective order (english only):
0 - used for articles and demonstratives (those, this, these)
1 - opinion
2 - size
3 - shape
4 - age
5 - color
6 - origin
7 - material
8 - purpose
